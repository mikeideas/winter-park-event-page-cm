What To Expect

Join a Monday meeting

Whether you are a prospective member or a long-time Rotarian, weekly meetings are a place to connect, grow, and serve.

  • Networking Opportunities Build meaningful relationships with community leaders and professionals.
  • City Updates Stay informed about local developments and initiatives in Winter Park.
  • Inspiring Talks Hear from influential leaders and neighbors sharing insight, experience, and vision.
  • Lunch Buffet Enjoy a fresh buffet for $29. Lunch is optional and not required to attend.
